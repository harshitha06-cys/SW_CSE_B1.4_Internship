Box Model: Tells us how every element on a web page is treated as a rectangular box. 4 important concepts(inside to outside): 1. content : text/image/video | width x height| 2. Padding : space between content & border | transparent but it takes white-space 3. Border : Line around the padding & content 4. Margin : space outside the border, b/w the current element & other elements
